Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How do I create an account?
A: You can create an account by clicking on the Sign Up button and providing your email address and password.

Q: How can I reset my password?
A: Click on the Forgot Password link and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Q: How many days return policy is available?
A: Customers can return products within 7 days of delivery.

Q: When will I receive my refund?
A: Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after the returned product is picked up.

Q: What should I do if I receive a damaged product?
A: If the product is damaged, you can request a return or replacement from the Orders section.
