Return Policy

Customers are allowed to return products within 7 days from the date of delivery.

Returned products must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging.

If a damaged or defective product is received, customers are eligible for a full refund or replacement.

Once the returned item is picked up, the refund will be initiated within 5 to 7 business days.

Certain products such as personal care items are not eligible for return.
